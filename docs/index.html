Bottom Line
This is a solid but not exceptional update — underlying earnings growth (ex the one-off) is respectable but EBITDA margin is quietly declining and the headline NPAT number is being inflated by an accounting adjustment management chose to lead with. The H2 softness, deferred revenues, and wide guidance range suggest some execution risk in the final weeks; investors should anchor to the lower end of the range until audited numbers confirm.
